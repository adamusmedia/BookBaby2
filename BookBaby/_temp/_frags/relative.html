Your recipe for a successful cookbook

Design: Make sure your book cover stands out on the shelf with professional cover design and photography. The better the food looks, the more people will want to make it! Learn more about our design services.

Format: Plan for one recipe per spread. Doing so will allow for a larger font which can be read at arm’s length when in use in the kitchens of your readers.

Content: Instead of listing the recipes in alphabetical order, try dividing the recipes into sections by ingredients, type of entrée, etc. Your recipes should be clear to novice and expert cooks, alike.
